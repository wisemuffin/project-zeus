## About Project Zeus

Project Zeus is an Australian market research platform purpose-built for university
marketing teams. Our data pipeline ingests, standardises, and models public
government datasets — Jobs and Skills Australia (IVI), QILT, UAC, VTAC, SATAC, ABS,
CRICOS, and Google Trends — into actionable targeting intelligence.

The platform is built on open-source analytics infrastructure (Dagster, dbt,
DuckDB, Evidence.dev), updated automatically from source data, and delivered as
interactive dashboards with full data source attribution.
