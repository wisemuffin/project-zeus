---
title: UK University Marketing Opportunities
---

UK market research insights to help universities optimise digital marketing campaigns for student acquisition. Data pipeline under development.

**Status:** Pipeline scaffolding complete. Data assets being built.

<Details title="Planned Data Sources">

- **UCAS** — sector-level admissions data by subject group, domicile, and demographics
- **ONS** — labour market demand (vacancies and employment by SOC occupation group)
- **HESA** — Graduate Outcomes Survey (employment rates and salaries by subject)
- **ONS / Nomis** — population estimates by Local Authority District (LAD)
- **Google Trends** — trending searches and university brand interest in the UK

</Details>
